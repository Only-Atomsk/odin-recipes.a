Carne Asada

Describtion:

A juicy and flavorful carne asada starts with a delicious carne asada marinade. This recipe begins with a 24-hour marinade to allow the flank steak to soak up all the tasty and fresh flavors before grilling.

Whether you serve it as tacos, a salad topping, or simply chow down on the tender, flavor-packed steak by itself, this will be the best carne asada you've ever experienced.

Ingredients:

  • Fruit Juice: Orange juice, lemon juice, and lime juice add fruity flavor to this marinade.
  • Soy Sauce: This marinade includes cilantro, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, pepper, and oregano.
  • Chipotle Pepper: The finely chopped chipotle pepper adds a kick of heat.
  • Olive Oil: Olive oil keeps the steak moist and helps to lock in the flavors.

Steps:

  1. Combine Ingredients
  2. Place steak in a bowl with martinade
  3. Grill
  4. Slice and serve
